Wisp: Echoes of the Afterlight

Project information

  • Category: Video Game
  • Team: Soul Games
  • Project date: 03 March, 2024
  • Project URL: https://ruumien.itch.io/wisp

Embark on a mystical adventure in "Wisp," a 2D pixel-art game set in a captivating underworld. As a newly appointed Grim Reaper, guide lost souls to peace and uncover the secrets of a corrupt realm. With a unique time-stopping watch, engage in strategic gameplay, face challenging boss battles, and navigate a dynamic economy of coins and points. Delve into a world of adventure, puzzles, and fantasy storytelling, where every choice shapes your path. Unravel the mysteries of the afterlife, one soul at a time, in "Wisp."
